About Me

Greetings, I'm Isai Chaidez, a dedicated student and an ardent enthusiast of life's wonders. My journey has led me to explore the realms of Vedic philosophy, a path rich in wisdom and holistic practices. From yoga and meditation to breathwork and herbalism, I've immersed myself in these ancient arts that nurture both body and mind.

My educational pursuit has been as diverse as my passions. Armed with a Master's degree in Ayurvedic Sciences and a Bachelor's degree in Herbal Sciences, I've delved into the intricacies of natural health. This academic foundation fuels my desire to integrate these principles into the digital realm.

As a coding student at Code Fellows in Seattle, I'm excited about the boundless possibilities that technology offers. My aim is to fuse my knowledge of pranayamas with the digital landscape, envisioning a watchOS app that seamlessly incorporates these transformative practices into daily life. I believe in the power of technology to create meaningful experiences that enhance well-being.
